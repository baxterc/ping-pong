Ping Pong!

What does this thing do?

This page will show a list of the numbers from 1 up to a number that you enter, unless the number:

  • is a multiple of 3, in which case it becomes "Ping!"
  • is a multiple of 5, in which case it becomes "Pong!"
  • is a multiple of both 3 and 5, in which case it becomes "Pingpong!"
